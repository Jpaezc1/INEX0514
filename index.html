About INEX Construction East Coast

Full-service construction support from concept to completion.

INEX Construction East Coast is a full-service construction and design-support company specializing in residential and commercial projects across the Southeast. Our capabilities include 3D modeling and high-end project renderings, permitting assistance, engineered drawing coordination with qualified professionals as required, repairs, remodeling, restoration, and full construction/build services.

From concept development and visualization to permit coordination and final construction, we help clients plan, understand, and complete projects with clear communication and reliable execution.
